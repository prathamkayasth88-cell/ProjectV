What is Responsibility?

Responsibility means being accountable for your actions and duties. As a student, it means:

✅ School Responsibility

  • Completing homework on time
  • Attending classes regularly
  • Respecting teachers

🏠 Home Responsibility

  • Helping with chores
  • Keeping your room clean
  • Taking care of belongings

🎯 Personal Responsibility

  • Managing your time
  • Being honest
  • Learning from mistakes

💡 Quick Quiz: Are you Responsible?

Do you complete your homework before playing?
