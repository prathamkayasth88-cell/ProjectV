Golden Rules of Civic Sense

Civic sense means caring for your community and environment. Follow these rules:

1

Keep it Clean

Don't litter. Use dustbins everywhere.

2

Respect Others

Don't make noise in public places or libraries.

3

Follow Rules

Wait for your turn in queues. Obey traffic rules.

4

Help Others

Offer your seat to elders. Help those in need.

5

Save Resources

Switch off lights and fans when not needed.
